
# RouteIQ 🚍🧠

### Smarter Journeys. Better Decisions.

RouteIQ is an AI-powered public transit companion that helps commuters choose the best route, compare costs, track vehicles live, and receive real-time journey assistance.

---

## 🚀 What It Does

* Finds the **Fastest**, **Cheapest**, and **Most Comfortable** routes
* Uses AI to recommend the smartest option
* Compares Transit vs Uber/Ola/Rapido/Auto costs
* Dynamically replans routes during delays
* Supports Meet Friends and Midpoint planning
* Calculates money saved and CO₂ reduction

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧠 AI Route Recommendation

Explains why a route is best based on time, cost, reliability, and comfort.

### 🚍 Live Bus Tracking

Track vehicle location, ETA, and arrival status in real time.

### 🔄 Dynamic Replanning

Automatically suggests better routes when delays occur.

### 💰 Smart Alternatives

Compare public transit against ride-hailing platforms.

### ⏳ Miss-The-Bus Analysis

Shows how much time you'll lose if you miss a connection.

### 🤝 Meet Friends & Midpoint

Find the fairest meeting point for multiple people.

### 🔔 AI Journey Assistant

Departure reminders, delay alerts, and contextual nudges.

## 🎯 Core Innovation

Most apps stop after generating a route.

**RouteIQ continues helping throughout the journey** by monitoring transit conditions, predicting disruptions, and proactively recommending better decisions.
